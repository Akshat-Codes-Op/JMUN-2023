About Seth M.R. Jaipuria School

Seth M.R. Jaipuria School, Gomti Nagar, Lucknow began its journey in 1992. It is recognized as one of the creme-de-la-creme names in education in India which provides education from play group to grade 12. We are guided by the Philosophy of Education promulgated by Sri Aurobindo and The Mother. We believe in partnership with our parents to impart value based education to our children that would chisel them into good human beings, for education is not about getting 90% alone; it is about believing in and living the spirit of "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam"

Seth M.R. Jaipuria School

The school believes that all education is within so children are exposed to a wide range of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities to find their interests and perfection within. To develop the 21st century skills of analysis, innovation, creativity, problem solving, collaboration and communication, the school encourages student led initiatives. Some of the student led initiatives are Techvista, Elixir,Akshar Parva, Spoken word poetry, Book Forum, Creative Collaboration Club, Anandotsav, Mridang, Jhankar Beatz and JMUN. The interschool student led initiatives have found recognition among prominent schools of the country and are much awaited events.
